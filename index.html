About our Catering


Palermo, the vibrant capital city of Sicily, is renowned for its rich culinary heritage. From mouthwatering street food to hearty traditional dishes, the flavors of Palermo capture the essence of the region. In this article, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey as we introduce you to a catering service that brings the authentic taste of Palermo right to your table. Prepare to tantalize your taste buds with a menu that showcases Palermo's culinary gems.We only use fresh, organic ingredients.

Palermo, with its unique blend of history, culture, and gastronomy, offers a culinary experience like no other. Through our catering service, we aim to share the rich flavors and traditions of Palermo with you. From the crispy panelle to the creamy panacota, each dish on our menu has been carefully curated to provide an authentic taste of Palermo. Whether you're hosting a special event, a corporate gathering, or simply treating yourself to a flavorful meal, let our catering service transport you to the vibrant streets and hidden corners of Palermo, right from your own table. Our Menu


Panelle

Experience the true essence of Sicilian street food with panelle. These golden, deep-fried chickpea fritters are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. Served with a sprinkle of sea salt and a squeeze of fresh lemon, panelle will transport you to the bustling markets of Palermo. 10.50


Pizzette

Indulge in the miniature delights of pizzette, bite-sized Sicilian pizzas bursting with flavor. Topped with a variety of fresh ingredients such as tomatoes, olives, and capers, these little delights pack a punch and are perfect for any gathering. 7.00


Pasta Limone

Discover the refreshing flavors of pasta limone, a classic Sicilian pasta dish. Prepared with al dente pasta, zesty lemon zest, fragrant basil, and a sprinkle of Parmesan, this light yet flavorful dish will transport you to the sunny lemon groves of Palermo. 15.50


Carbonara

Savor the richness of our carbonara, a dish that pays homage to the timeless combination of flavors from the Palermitan countryside. With perfectly cooked spaghetti, crispy guanciale, creamy egg sauce, and a generous sprinkle of Pecorino Romano cheese, this classic pasta dish will leave you wanting more. 15.50


Panacota

For a sweet finale, indulge in our velvety panacota. This delicate Italian dessert, made with fresh cream, vanilla bean, and a luscious berry coulis, is the perfect ending to a memorable meal. Each spoonful is a taste of pure bliss. 8.50
